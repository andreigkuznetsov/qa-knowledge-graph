# QA Knowledge Graph Manifesto

## Engineering Quality Beyond Testing

Quality is not a collection of tests.

Quality is understanding.

Requirements, rules, scenarios, APIs, implementations and tests are not
independent assets. They form a single engineering knowledge system.

## We believe

-   Knowledge is more valuable than documents.
-   Relationships are more valuable than isolated objects.
-   Explainability is mandatory.
-   Traceability should be automatic.
-   AI should assist engineers, not replace engineering judgement.

## We are not building

-   another test management system;
-   another documentation platform;
-   another bug tracker.

We are building the **Knowledge Layer** above existing engineering
tools.

## Graph Thinking

Every requirement affects business rules.

Every rule affects scenarios.

Every scenario affects tests.

Every test affects releases.

Understanding these dependencies is the foundation of quality
engineering.

## Our Mission

> Build the Knowledge Layer for Software Quality Engineering.
